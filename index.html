About Us

Welcome to Jagdamba Chai Express, where every cup tells a story. What began as a small tea stall near MES Abasaheb Garware College has blossomed into a beloved spot for tea lovers. With our dedication to quality and tradition, we serve a variety of teas, coffees, and snacks that are sure to brighten your day.

We believe that the best conversations happen over a cup of chai, and we are here to make every sip special. Join us for a taste of our local favorites, made with love and care.
